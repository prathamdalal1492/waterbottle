Problems + Response

Our Problem:

Trash. All humans have different Opinions on a series of topics, but it can be Unanimous that all of us create Trash. However not all of us dispose of it correctly, and that can lead to significant environmental hazards. A major pollutant is water bottles, which if left in place release up to 13.2 pounds of Carbon Dioxide. Our group tried to find a way to encourage people to recycle more. In a survey of High School Students we discovered 37% of students do not know that if you recycle a water bottle you are compensated $0.05. Our goal was to use this key data point to come up with an efficent solution.

Our Solution

High Schoolers are desperate for money, there is a reason high school summer jobs are so desirable. they are an instantaneous way to make money. Our group created a better way to make money. We created an Automatic Water Bottle recycler that dispenses money on the spot. We believe this would encourage additional people to recycle. In a survey of High School Student 86% reported they would dispose of water bottles correctly if they were to get some form of compensation. Water Bottles will now be off the street and high schoolers will be happy.
